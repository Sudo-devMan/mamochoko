About us

Mamochoko Secondary School, situated at Stand 406, Moletjie, Ga-Kobo Village, operates as a public secondary school within the Ga-Kobo, Polokwane rural suburb of Limpopo Province, South Africa. It falls under the Capricorn North District, Bahlaloga circuit.

Birth of Mamochoko >

Mamochoko-Secondary-School was established in 2016 by community members who wanted to reduce the distance that their children took when they travelled to school. The school was able to advance at a very fast pace, and it were able to recruit a team of expert teachers that provide quality teaching.

Fun fact(s) >

  • 1. The name of our school is actually an acronym that stands for the 4 villages that are situated near and/or around the school.
    Ma - Mashita
    mo - Mokgonyana
    cho - Chokoe
    ko - Kobo
